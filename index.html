Quick Help for Light Weight Kanban Board

  • Create new project: Use "n" or click the New link in the top navigation.
  • Save a project: Press enter or click "save".
  • Discard changes: Click "cancel", click anywhere outside the editing box or press "Esc".
  • Change order: Just drag the card to a new position.
